Research Profile
======
Postdoctoral researcher specialising in **Interpretable Machine Learning, Explainable AI, and AI for Business**, with a strong track record of world-class publications (first-author ABS/AJG 4 Journal ×6, ICML ×1, ICLR Spotlight ×1) and international award-winning research in interpretable AI. My research develops transparent, human-readable AI models—grounded in symbolic regression and automated feature engineering—that make complex decisions auditable and actionable for business and operations contexts. I have applied these methods in financial services (Ant Financial) and healthcare, while actively maintaining open-source AI tools with 150+ community stars.

Biography
======
Hengzhe Zhang received the B.Sc. degree in software engineering from Xiangtan University (Double First-Class University), Hunan, China, in 2019 and the M.Sc. degree in computer science from East China Normal University (Double First-Class University), Shanghai, China, in 2022. He received the Ph.D. degree in artificial intelligence from Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand, in 2025. He is currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in Artificial Intelligence at Victoria University of Wellington. His research interests include interpretable machine learning, explainable AI, symbolic regression, genetic programming, evolutionary computation, and automated machine learning.
